About us

The Nigerians in Diaspora Organisation Greece is the an arm of a global Nigerian Diaspora network. Established in the year 2000 and registered in England and Wales in 2002 under the Companies Act.

Our Objectives

To bring Nigerians living in the United Kingdom together and to identify those willing to offer their skills to assist Nigeria’s national development.

Our Vision

At NIDO Greece, our central vision is to bring Nigerians living in Greece together and to identify those willing to offer their skills to assist Nigeria’s national development. Which will be needed in developmental projects.
